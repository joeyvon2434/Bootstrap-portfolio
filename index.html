About Me

Traveller, runner, chemical engineer, skier, energy industry professional, and budding web developer. I'm a goofy person, and I firmly believe you can have fun even when you're doing something serious. I don’t like to say no to opportunities, and I try to create them wherever I can.

I then started a career in the oil industry that took me to Louisiana and Montana. When I wanted to do something at the leading edge of technology, I knew it was time to make a career change. I took the opportunity between my careers to fulfill a few of dreams. Ski a ton, travel, live overseas, and learn a new language. Ich spreche ein bisschen Deutsch. Now I’m in Orlando learning new skills and searching for a new industry. For more information, please see my detailed bio!
